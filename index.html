♻️ Waste Awareness Guide
🥬
WET WASTE

Biodegradable

  • Food scraps, vegetable peels, fruit waste
  • Leftover food, tea & coffee grounds
  • Flowers, leaves, garden trimmings
  • Dispose: Green bin — compost/biogas
  • Makes excellent organic fertilizer
📦
DRY WASTE

Recyclable

  • Paper, cardboard, newspapers
  • Plastic bottles, bags (clean)
  • Glass bottles, metal cans
  • Dispose: Blue bin — recycling
  • Clean before placing — no food residue
💻
E-WASTE

Electronic

  • Old phones, laptops, chargers
  • Batteries — never in regular bins!
  • CFL bulbs, circuit boards
  • Dispose: E-waste drop centres
  • Contains toxic metals — handle carefully
